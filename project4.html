The Challenge

The challenge was to design a feature article for an upcoming review of Dune: Prophecy that captures the epic and nuanced themes of the series while aligning with the editorial style of Vanity Fair.It required integrating futuristic and cinematic elements without overshadowing the textâ€™s readability or editorial purpose.

The Solution

Using a dual-tone blue and gold gradient background, the design established the thematic contrast. The younger character represented chaos with darker tones, while the older character embodied control with golden lighting. A futuristic block font for the title reinforced the sci-fi tone, and careful text placement ensured clarity and balance within the spread.
